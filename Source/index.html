Introduction

Virtual Reality (VR) is a new trend for gaming. VR gaming is becoming more and more popular. It is believed that VR games will be one of the main types of games in the not-too-distant future. There are many types of VR games, for example, puzzle, sport, simulation, party game, FPS (First-person shooter), social, etc. Some of them are multiplayer games.

Why it is valuable?

VR is an excellent platform for people who do not play video game very much (e.g. Kids and the elderly) because of the intuitive control in VR game remove the barrier of the traditional complicated video game. In this game, players will need to exchange their information to solve the puzzle, the communication skill of the players can thus be enhanced. In addition, puzzle-solving can train kidsâ€™ creative thinking and problem-solving skill; for the elderly, it may also help to relieve dementia.
